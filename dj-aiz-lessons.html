Phase 1 The Foundation
01
Day 1
Equipment & Setup Mastery
+
🎯 Core Objective
Getting your gear and software "gig-ready."
🛠 Techniques
Verify Master/Headphone outputs and set all EQ knobs to the 12 o'clock "neutral" position.
🔀 The Transition
Learn the "Crossfader Method" — the simplest way to move from Track A to Track B.
▶Watch: How to DJ for Beginners
02
Day 2
Manual Blending & EQ Control
+
🎯 Core Objective
Moving beyond the crossfader for professional, "invisible" transitions.
🛠 Techniques
Use volume faders to blend tracks and reduce the "Low" EQ knob of the outgoing track to prevent muddy bass.
✅ Verification
Use the headphone cue button to verify beat alignment before the crowd hears it.
▶Watch: How to DJ for Beginners
Phase 2 Muscle Memory & Flow
03
Day 3
The 10x Repetition Cycle
+
🎯 Core Objective
Building instinctive speed through "Intro-to-Outro" drills.
🛠 Techniques
Mix the intro of Song B into the outro of Song A. Reset EQs/BPM and swap back. Repeat 10 times.
🏆 Goal
Achieve flawless beatmatching and level matching without overthinking.
▶Watch: Fast Results Practice
04
Day 4
High-Energy Transitions
+
🎯 Core Objective
Learning "Drop Mixing" to keep energy levels peaked.
🛠 Techniques
Practice switching the current song's chorus for the upcoming song's chorus instantly on the "drop."
✨ Flair
Add backspins, filter sweeps, or echo-outs to clean up your mix-outs.
▶Watch: Fast Results Practice
Phase 3 The Professional Edge
05
Day 5
Harmonic Theory (The T-Method)
+
🎯 Core Objective
Ensuring every transition sounds musically perfect.
🛠 Techniques
Use the Camelot Wheel or the "Traffic Light" system to find compatible keys.
💡 Concept
Learn why mixing in key prevents dissonant clashes during long blends.
▶Watch: 10 Must-Know Techniques
06
Day 6
Pro-Level EQ & FX Swaps
+
🎯 Core Objective
Sophisticated frequency management.
🛠 Techniques
Execute "Level 3" blends by simultaneously managing filters, reverb, and MIDI EQ swaps.
🔊 The Bass Wall
Learn to swap the low-end frequencies exactly as a new musical phrase begins.
▶Watch: 10 Must-Know Techniques
07
Day 7
Creative Stems & Advanced Loops
+
🎯 Core Objective
Live performance remixing.
🛠 Techniques
Use "Stems" to isolate vocals for live mashups and master the James Hype-style loop tightening trick.
🎛 Precision
Use the jog wheel to adjust loop "in/out" points for a rising tension effect.
